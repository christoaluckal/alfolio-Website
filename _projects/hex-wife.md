Hex-Wife was developed as a low-cost autonomous UAV system for reliable point-to-point missions. The platform was part of the Mavericks UAS team effort and served as the primary aircraft for SUAS 2019.

## Roles

- Design Head
- Communications Operator

## Outcome

At AUVSI SUAS 2019, the team received the [Just Joe Sportsmanship Award](https://static1.squarespace.com/static/5d554e14aaa5e300011a4844/t/5d56ab1a239e2600017b00ee/1565960989653/auvsi_suas-2019-awards_banquet.pdf#page=20).
