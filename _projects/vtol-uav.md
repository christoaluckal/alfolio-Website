This project focused on designing and building a hybrid UAV with vertical take-off and landing capability around a high-wing trainer aircraft. The system targeted practical autonomous navigation with a pilot retained as a fallback operator.

## Role

Fabrication team.

## Outcome

The platform demonstrated reliable operational capability after repeated testing and served as part of a larger student UAV effort.
